SAN FRANCISCO, CA: A Chihuahua on the lam gave Bay Area California Highway Patrol officers a run for their money early Sunday morning. According to a tweet from the CHP San Francisco division, the little black dog was found running in the eastbound lanes of the Bay Bridge around 6:56 a.m. After a short pursuit, which was caught on tape by one of the CHP dashboard cameras, officers were able to safely rescue the dog.
